Integration of Google Email Client in Django

The project looks into how you can use Gmail as an Email Client for your website, this can be done when one needs a reliable and safe email client service. Also compounded by the fact that alot of organisation host their email accounts on Google's Gsuite due to its reliability.

The project will display two use cases, the first being - sending an email to another user and the second being - requesting for a password reset, where the reset link will be emailed to the user.

Sending Emails from the Web App

Where to start:

  • Sign up to create an account, use a valid email during sign up
  • Log out
  • Sign up to create a second account, use a valid email during sign up
  • Log in using either of the accounts
  • Click on "Compose Email" to compose an email
  • A page with a drop down list of registered will be displayed
  • Select the user you want to send an email to
  • Enter a subject
  • Compose a message
  • Click on "Send" to send the email
  • You will be notified once the email is successfully sent
  • Log into the email account for the user you selected
  • You should see the email that was sent in the "Inbox" or "Spam" folder

Requesting for a Password Reset

Where to start:

  • Click on the "Log In" menu at the top right corner
  • If you're logged in, log out and go to the "Log In" page
  • Sign up to create a second account, use a valid email during sign up
  • On the "Log In" page click on "Forgot Password?" link
  • A "Reset Password page will be displayed for you to enter your email address
  • Enter the email address of one of the registered users
  • Log into the email account for the user you selected
  • You should see a request for password reset email in the "Inbox" or "Spam" folder
  • This email will have a link to it, click on the link
  • It will take you to a page for you to enter your new password
  • Once you have entered and submited the form, it will take you to a page confirming the password reset was a success
  • Log in to try the new password
